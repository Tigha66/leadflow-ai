## 📋 Email Sequence Strategy

## 🎯 Target Research Methodology

### Finding Decision Makers:
1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** Search by industry, location, company size
2. **Google Search:** "[Service] [City] owner" or "CEO"
3. **Better Business Bureau:** Business listings with contact info
4. **Chamber of Commerce:** Member directories
5. **Industry Associations:** Member lists

### Email Finding Tools:
- Hunter.io for email discovery
- Clearbit Connect for contact info
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Voila Norbert for verification

## 📊 Tracking & Metrics

### KPIs to Monitor:
- **Open Rate:** Target >25%
- **Click Rate:** Target >3%
- **Reply Rate:** Target >1%
- **Demo Rate:** Target >15% of replies
- **Conversion Rate:** Target >25% of demos

### A/B Testing Elements:
- Subject lines (power words, curiosity, personalization)
- Send times (Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM)
- Personalization level (name, business, pain point)
- Call-to-action (demo, call, video)

## 📞 Phone Follow-Up Strategy

### After Email Sequence:
1. **Call 1:** Introduction, value proposition
2. **Call 2:** Address objections, case studies
3. **Call 3:** Demo offer, trial proposal

### Voicemail Scripts:
"Hi [Name], [Your Name] from LeadFlow AI. I sent you an email about recovering missed leads and increasing bookings. I've helped service businesses like yours increase appointments by 67%. If you're interested in hearing how, give me a call back at [number]. Have a great day!"

## 🎣 Compliance & Best Practices

### CAN-SPAM Compliance:
- Clear identification as advertisement
- Valid physical address
- Easy unsubscribe mechanism
- Accurate subject lines
- Honorable opt-out requests promptly

### Deliverability Tips:
- Warm up email accounts gradually
- Use double opt-in for lists
- Maintain good sender reputation
- Personalize content appropriately
- Test across email clients
